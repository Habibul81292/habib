3 Filter by "Pizza" category
4 Check ratings & reviews

08 / 15 🌳 Algorithm

Decision Tree Algorithm

Works like a flowchart — yes/no questions

🌳

How It Works

A Decision Tree makes decisions by asking a series of yes/no questions.

❓ Is product in stock? → Yes / No
❓ Is price affordable? → Yes / No
❓ Has customer bought before? → Yes / No
✅ Final: Will customer purchase?
Business Example

📊 Decision Flow

Will Customer Purchase?
✅ In Stock
✅ Affordable
PURCHASE ✓
❌ Out of Stock
❌ High Price
NO PURCHASE ✗

10 / 15 🗺️ Pathfinding

Dijkstra & A* Algorithms

Finding the shortest and fastest paths

📍

Dijkstra Algorithm

Finds the shortest path between two points in a graph.

Start → A (5) → C (2) → D (4) → End (1)
Total: 5 + 2 + 4 + 1 = 12
Google Maps Rapido
✨

A* Algorithm

Smarter, faster pathfinding using heuristics (educated guesses).

A* = g(n) + h(n)
g(n) = actual distance · h(n) = estimate to goal
Navigation Apps Gaming
